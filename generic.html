Cohort
Analysis

I was recently tasked with creating a cohort analysis on a group of retailers to monitor their customer credit behaviour. The goal of the analysis was to identify the first instance in which customers had gone 3 or more months without making payments on their accounts and at which point in time this happened. We wanted to see if there would be trends and patterns in our analysis. We based our analysis on a 24 month period to get the most accurate results.

Code Snippet: SQL

SELECT
subscriberGroup,
AltSubsGroupName,
AppStream,
Industry_new,
CalYearQuarter,
FirstArrears,
NumberFirstArrears,
SUM(NumberFirstArrears) OVER(PARTITION BY AltSubsGroupName,CalYearQuarter ORDER BY AltSubsGroupName,CalYearQuarter,CAST(FirstArrears AS INT)) AS CumArrears

INTO QuarterlyCumArrears
FROM QuarterlyNumFirstArrears

ORDER BY AltSubsGroupName,CalYearQuarter,CAST(FirstArrears AS INT)
